Our Programs

Nurturing Young Minds, Building Strong Foundations

Infants (0-12 months)

Our infant program provides a nurturing and safe environment where babies can explore and develop at their own pace. We focus on:

  • Sensory development activities
  • Motor skills development
  • Individual care routines
  • Safe and stimulating environment

Toddlers (1-3 years)

Our toddler program encourages exploration and independence through:

  • Interactive play activities
  • Basic language development
  • Social skills building
  • Creative expression

Preschool (3-5 years)

Preparing your child for kindergarten through:

  • Early literacy and numeracy
  • Science and discovery
  • Art and music
  • Physical development
